The Ultimate Trading Platform

  • Reliability

    Herald Forex places extreme emphasis on the reliability of its trading platform and performs stringent testing on all software and infrastructure components prior to production release. Clients benefit from the highly skilled team of Quality Assurance personnel that continuously monitors platform operations and functions along with technical support that is available 24/7/365.

  • Custom-made

    Because no two Forex firms are the same, Herald Forex has developed a modular approach to its trading platforms. Every platform is configured to each firm’s needs and is extremely flexible with hundreds of user-definable functions and parameters. Business Development Specialist and Client Services personnel provide expert guidance through each phase of enterprise setup and subsequent operations.

  • Managed Service Provider

    Using Herald Forex services gives your company multiple advantages over developing a proprietary in-house platform and still allows you to retain control of your firm’s most important business functions. Time-tested software reliability combined with Herald Forex MSP attributes gives your firm advantages of 24 hour IT support, free semi-annual software updates with the latest tools, and hosted by Herald Forex using the SAS-70 server.

  • Independence

    Herald Forex brings a new meaning to independence. It frees your firm from the rigors of platform maintenance and development, leaving them to a highly experienced service provider. This gives your firm the chance to focus more on its strategy and business goals. Our developers work around the clock to provide you with the most up to date tools to reach a wide span of clients and meet their demands.

  • Cost Free Customization

    All Herald Forex platforms feature extensive user-definable business rules allowing the client to adjust various aspects of the platform: instruments and feed sources, liquidity providers, look and feel, certain end-user specific features, and various dealing desk parameters. This accommodates handling most client customization requests rapidly, without coding and without charge. Beyond that, Herald Forex frequently accommodates requests for client-specific platform modifications, often at no extra cost.

  • Ease of Entry

    Regardless of the size of your firm, you can be in business in as little as five days with an Herald Forex turnkey solution. An affordable setup fee enables your company to bypass many of the major expenses involved in setting up a trading platform including hosting the application at a SAS-70 secure data center, IT costs, all of the software development, updates, technical support and much more, making it extremely easy to enter this lucrative market.

  • Economics

    Developing and designing proprietary software, maintaining a secure data center along with an IT department to manage it, and hiring top-notch computer professionals to be on call 24/7 is an enormous cost by any standard. In fact these expenses are such a huge barrier-to-entry that only a handful of large corporations are capable of such a mission. Herald Forex best-practices solutions make it extremely affordable for your company to enter into and remain competitive in the retail Forex industry.
